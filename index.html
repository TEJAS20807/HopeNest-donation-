They Need Your Help to Live

Together, we can create a community where no family is left behind. Your contribution brings hope, support, and a brighter future for those in need.



       Together, We Can Change a Life       

          Every day, thousands of families struggle for basic necessities—food, shelter, medicine, and education.
           Your small act of kindness can bring hope to a family that is fighting to survive.


        Why Your Support Matters

          Many poor families face:
          Lack of daily meals
          No stable income
          Children missing school
          Health issues due to no medical aid
          Your donation helps provide essential support and gives them a chance to live with dignity..

Sita paid her daughter’s school fees, grew her business, and improved her home

“As any single mother, all I thought of was how I would provide for my children. With the second transfer, I bought iron sheets, a bicycle and clothes for my last born in secondary school. My greatest achievement so far has been paying school fees for my daughter. For the first time, I have paid it in full.”

RAJASTHAN

Kisan invested in a farming business and restarted construction on his home

“When I received the money, I invested Rs.800 into farming. Since I don’t have a farm land here, I asked a land owner for over a lot to plant cassava. I paid people to brush, clean, and plant. The rest of the money, I traveled to Village where I have a half lot with a foundation that stopped several years ago because I did not have the money to continue with the project.”

UTTAR PRADESH

Pushpa expanded her flower stall and supported her kids

“I put the funds into buying more flowers and vegetables to sell. I paid my children’s school fees, and we finally fixed our leaking roof. Now our stall attracts more customers, and our lives have improved.”

HIMACHAL PRADESH
